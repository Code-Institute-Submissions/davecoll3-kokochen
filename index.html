About Me

In January 2020, I started a course in illustration and graphic design with the aim of making a career change. My aim is to work in illustration for children’s literature and to write and illustrate my own stories. I am currently a bilingual (French/English) freelance writer, specialising in film. My skills and training allow me to offer my clients greater control of the work I am asked to produce, both editorially and graphically.
